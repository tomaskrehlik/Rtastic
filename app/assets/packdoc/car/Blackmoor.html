Exercise Histories of Eating-Disordered and Control Subjects

Usage

Blackmoor

Description

The Blackmoor data frame has 945 rows and 4 columns. Blackmoor and Davis's data on exercise histories of 138 teenaged girls hospitalized for eating disorders and 98 control subjects.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns: subjecta factor with subject id codes. ageage in years. exercisehours per week of exercise. groupa factor with levels: control, Control subjects; patient, Eating-disordered patients.
