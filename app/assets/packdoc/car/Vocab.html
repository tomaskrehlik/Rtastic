Vocabulary and Education

Usage

Vocab

Description

The Vocab data frame has 21,638 rows and 5 columns. The observations are respondents to U.S. General Social Surveys, 1972-2004.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns: yearYear of the survey. sexSex of the respondent, Female or Male. education Education, in years.

vocabulary Vocabulary test score: number correct on a 10-word test.
