Cowles and Davis's Data on Volunteering

Usage

Cowles

Description

The Cowles data frame has 1421 rows and 4 columns. These data come from a study of the personality determinants of volunteering for psychological research.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns: neuroticismscale from Eysenck personality inventory extraversionscale from Eysenck personality inventory sexa factor with levels: female; male volunteervolunteeing, a factor with levels: no; yes
