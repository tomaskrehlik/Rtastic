Agricultural Production in Mazulu Village

Usage

Sahlins

Description

The Sahlins data frame has 20 rows and 2 columns. The observations are households in a Central African village.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns: consumers Consumers/Gardener, ratio of consumers to productive individuals.

acres Acres/Gardener, amount of land cultivated per gardener.
