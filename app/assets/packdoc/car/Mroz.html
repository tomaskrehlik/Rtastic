U.S. Women's Labor-Force Participation

Usage

Mroz

Description

The Mroz data frame has 753 rows and 8 columns. The observations, from the Panel Study of Income Dynamics (PSID), are married women.

Format

This data frame contains the following columns: lfplabor-force participation; a factor with levels: no; yes.

k5number of children 5 years old or younger. k618number of children 6 to 18 years old. agein years. wcwife's college attendance; a factor with levels: no; yes.

hchusband's college attendance; a factor with levels: no; yes.

lwglog expected wage rate; for women in the labor force, the actual wage rate; for women not in the labor force, an imputed value based on the regression of lwg on the other variables. incfamily income exclusive of wife's income.
